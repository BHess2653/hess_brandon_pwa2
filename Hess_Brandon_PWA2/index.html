Join Us

Join our group and play games with us. We play GTA V and Destiny on Xbox and we're always looking for more team members to have fun with and we help eachother out whether it be heists and raids or achievements and collectibles. We have a list of when we're online and if your a member you can list your own game times.

Destiny

On Destiny we help team members with any missions or strikes they may need help on. Later during the week we do Nightfalls and weekly heroics. Then during the weekend we do Raids. We also do PVP when team members are free.

GTA V

On GTA V we have fun online and play on multiple playlists. We help eachother earn Money and RP points through playing the mission playlist. We also do Heists with any members who are up for it.
